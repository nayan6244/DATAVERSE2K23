GENERAL RULES

•Only one team can participate for an event from a department and students participating in more than one event should make sure that the events don’t clash.
•It is mandated that all participants bring their College ID and Bonafide certificate from their HOD/Principal.
•ON SPOT registration starts from 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM (MCA BLOCK GROUND FLOOR).
•No participants will be allowed inside the college after 8.15 AM as college gates will be closed.
•Participants if once went outside the premises of the college will not be allowed inside again.
•Participants have to make their own arrangements for Lunch and Refreshments. College Canteen will open on the day.
•Dress code: Boys – Formal shirts/ Colored T Shirt, Full Pant (Round neck T Shirts, Torn Jeans are not allowed) Girls – Formal dress (Short tops are not allowed)
•Prize distribution will be held only during the Valedictory Function (03:00 PM)
•Venue (Registration desk) - In Front of Data science lab, MCA block (Ground floor)

  • YOSIPOMA (let our ideas meet) - PAPER PRESENTATION

    Timing: 09:30 am - 11:30 am

    1. Individual or team participation (with maximum 2 participants per team)
    2. First 6 minutes is allocated for project explanation followed by a 1 minute Q&A session
    3. Mail the abstract of your project to dataverse2k23@gmail.com by 12th December
    4. The topics of projects should be chosen on your own (topics should be related to data analytics, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, etc.)
    5. Bring your own gadgets
    6. Presentation time restrictions will be strictly followed. A buzzer will be played 1 minute before the 6 minutes allotted for presentation
    7. Decision of the judges shall be final and abiding for all the events

    Phone: 9843782540 (Maria Jesu Raja)

  • SHARK TANK

    Timing: 01:30 P.M – 02:30 P.M

    The participants have to pitch their startup ideas related to the data science domain or a business idea that is well integrated with AI
    1. Maximum 3 participants per team
    2. There will be only one round
    3. Teams can come up with any business model or idea only based on Tech fields
    4. The model can be product based or service based
    5. Try to come up with interesting and excitingly new ideas
    6. Each team will have 8-10 min of time to explain their canvas and to sell their product
    7. Every team should prepare a PPT to explain their idea with alongside of their canvas
    8. Best 3 ideas will be awarded with top 3 places
    9. Decision of the judges shall be final and abiding for all the events

    For further queries contact
    Phone: 7306705995 (Husna Mundambra )

  • VISUAL FUSION - Data visualization

    Timing:Prelims: 10.30 A.M to 11.00 A.M
    Finals: 11.15 A.M to 12.00 P.M

    This competition aims to assess students’ creativity through the telling of a self-contained story, therefore participants will be required to construct a single narrative from the facts, draw their own conclusions, and visually express those conclusions.


    1. 2 participants per team
    2. Teams should complete the given task within 40 minutes
    3. The goal is to produce the most imaginative visualization and detailed narrative description from the data
    4. The participants should work on PowerBI, Tableau, Python, R, SPSS
    5. Before the competition begins, players who wants to use other platforms must obtain authorization for the relevant platforms
    6. Decision of the judges shall be final and abiding for all the events

    For further queries contact
    Phone: 9952092552 (Mesibun)
